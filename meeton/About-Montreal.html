About Montreal

Montreal is...

McMUN takes place in the beautiful city of Montréal, Quebec. A diverse and multicultural metropolis, Montreal is constantly buzzing with life, even in the heart of winter. From the bars and clubs on Saint-Laurent and the cobble-stoned streets of Old Montreal, to the cultural and culinary happenings that take place all over the city, Montreal’s unique vibe combines European class and North American energy to a create a distinct Montréalais atmosphere that’s unlike anywhere else. The conference will take place in a hotel ideally situated in downtown Montreal, steps away from the best nightlife and sights the city has to offer. McMUN thus represents the perfect opportunity to explore the city between committee sessions.
